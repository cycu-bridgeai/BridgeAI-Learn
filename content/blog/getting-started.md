## Why AI Learning Matters

Artificial intelligence is reshaping every industry. Whether you're a developer, designer, or educator, understanding the fundamentals gives you a real edge.

## Where to Begin

Start with the basics before diving into frameworks and tools:

1. **Linear algebra** — vectors, matrices, and transformations
2. **Probability & statistics** — how models reason under uncertainty
3. **Python** — the language of modern AI/ML

## A Simple Learning Path

### Week 1–2: Foundations
Focus on math concepts. Khan Academy's linear algebra series is free and excellent.

### Week 3–4: Python for Data Science
Learn NumPy, Pandas, and basic visualization with Matplotlib.

### Week 5–8: Machine Learning Basics
Work through scikit-learn tutorials. Build a simple classifier on a real dataset.
