## About this Video

Yannic Kilcher walks through the original *"Attention Is All You Need"* paper (Vaswani et al., 2017) in detail. This is the paper that introduced the transformer architecture.

## Why It Matters

Before this paper:
- State-of-the-art NLP used RNNs and LSTMs
- Long-range dependencies were a fundamental limitation
- Training was slow due to sequential processing

After:
- Attention allows parallel computation across the full sequence
- Transformers scaled to unprecedented sizes
- GPT, BERT, T5, and every modern LLM descend from this architecture
